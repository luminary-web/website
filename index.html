got questions? find answers here

our specialty lies in creating SaaS MVPs, design systems, consumer mobile apps, captivating framer landing pages, illustrations, and high-conversion websites. additionally, we excel in seamlessly collaborating with your design team to help you achieve your goals.

we partner with a select few design agencies to assist with their high-demand projects. through collaboration, we support them in meeting their project requirements.

we plan ahead before kicking off your project, ensuring we deliver designs in a timely manner. moreover, you can always add more tasks according to your needs, and we'll prioritize them after consulting with you or your team.

hiring a full-time senior-level designer can come with a hefty price tag, often exceeding $180,000 annually, plus benefits. additionally, finding one available can be quite a challenge. with our monthly plan, you enjoy the freedom to pause and resume your subscription as needed, ensuring you only pay for design work when you have tasks to complete. plus, we match designers to your specific needs based on their expertise. it's not easy to find a designer who excels in both design and has specialized experience in meeting your unique requirements, such as creating MVPs and enhancing ux for B2B SaaS, or crafting high-conversion websites.

you can always pause your subscription once you're done, and come back whenever you have more design needs.

we understand that design often involves iterations. we'll revise until you're completely satisfied with the results.

we get it – sometimes you might not have enough design tasks to keep you busy for the whole month. maybe you only have one or two design requests right now. that's where pausing your subscription can be really useful. our billing cycles run for 31 days. so, let's say you sign up and use the service for 21 days, then decide to hit the pause button. your billing cycle will pause too, and you'll still have 10 days of service left to use whenever you need it down the road.

clients often request designs through familiar platforms like linear, jira, slack or trello. they might also share google docs or wireframes, or even send a loom video. plus, if clients prefer, we're happy to sync up over a call to discuss their needs. once we've got the details, we'll set up tickets on linear or trello to keep track of tasks.

you'll collaborate closely with the heart of luminary – our core team. each member brings their own unique design expertise to the table, whether it's in B2B SaaS, consumer mobile apps, framer, webflow, user research, developer centric products, and more.

we offer framer and webflow services, and for certain clients, we also handle frontend and backend work using react/node.

since our business relies on human effort and time, we don't offer refunds.
